01

Principles

the voice of the system
i · clarity first

Content is the UI

No ornament. Hierarchy lives in weight, size, and space — not chrome, boxes, or colored fills.

ii · density with air

Scannable, readable

A researcher's page. Lots of information, each line earning its place; whitespace doing real work.

iii · restraint

One accent only

Neutrals carry 95% of the surface. A single cobalt does links, focus, and the rare highlight.
